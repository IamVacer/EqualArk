About Equal-ARK

To provide social, emotional, behavioral growth and development through Equine Assisted Learning, Equine Facilitated Psychotherapy and vocational guidance to youth in Singapore.

More

What is Equine Therapy

Studies and researchers around the world have obtained positive results that support Equine (family of horses, donkeys and ponies) Assisted Learning as a therapy tool. It promotes physical, occupational, emotional and social growth for youths at risk and/or those who have special needs.

What we do

EQUAL-ARK provides five programs catered for youths.

  • EQUAL: Character and Citizenship Education for specialised schools
  • After School: Extra-Curricular Activities for students
  • Scholars: Rehabilitative program for 'youths-at-risk'
  • EQUATE: Rehabilitative program for youths with special needs
  • Stables Workshop: Transition employment program to the community

Equine Academy

Our Equine Assisted Learning Centre

Situated within the National Equestrian Park, it comprises of a learning facilities building, horse stables and a covered arena. All learning is conducted within these three facilities by our qualified staff and horse instructors.

The building can also be converted to a veterinary surgical theatre and a viewing gallery to provide for learning opportunities for all our students. Whether the students are participating in the Equine Assisted Learning program or all our other vocational programs, all the requirements for Equine Therapy are catered for the students.
